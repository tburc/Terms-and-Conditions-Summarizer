    What you can do to protect your privacy:

    Take charge of your online privacy by reviewing and limiting app permissions, using privacy-focused browsers and search engines, enabling two-factor authentication, and adjusting social media privacy settings. Consider using a VPN to mask your location and encrypt traffic. Support privacy legislation by contacting your local representatives or mayor, and stay informed about your digital rights. Where possible, opt out of unnecessary data sharing and regularly review the privacy settings of your devices and accounts.
